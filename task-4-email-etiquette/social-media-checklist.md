

Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students
## The Do's
•Use privacy settings to strictly control who can see your personal posts and photos.
•Verify information and news sources before sharing them to avoid spreading
misinformation.
•Build a professional network by following industry leaders and joining academic groups.
•Use strong, unique passwords and enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on all
accounts.
•Think before you post, as digital footprints can impact future internship opportunities.
## The Don'ts
•Don't post sensitive data like your college ID, Aadhaar number, or home address.
•Don't engage in cyberbullying or use offensive language in public comments or threads.
•Don't post unprofessional content that you wouldn't want a future employer to see.
•Don't click on suspicious links or "too good to be true" offers in your social feeds.
•Don't share your real-time location on public profiles while you are away from home.
